About Me

I'm currently a postgraduate student at the London School of Economics studying the MSc in Data Science. Before this, I spent two years as an actuarial analyst in the insurance industry, where I worked on projects ranging from building statistical models for risk quantification to optimising processes and conducting data analyses to support strategic decision-making.

As a data scientist, I enjoy bridging the gap between data and decision-making by combining my technical expertise with a curiosity for uncovering meaningful insights. With vast amounts of data to explore, my goal is to interpret its story and help drive impactful decisions.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, you may find me playing basketball, table tennis or watching films.
